About Akshay

Akshay Jojo, born in September 2000, is a visionary product developer and researcher with a strong background in embedded systems and mechanical design. Since 2020, Akshay has been actively working as a product developer, gaining experience across various domains, including microcontrollers, sensors, circuit design, 3D printing, and soft robotics.

As the Founder of ORCI, Managing Director of GNI Labs and Fofus, Akshay has led innovative projects that push the boundaries of technology. His role as a Project Development Manager at Zaara Biotech LLC, underscores his expertise in project management and product development.

Driven by a mission to engineer products that hold both social and economic value, Akshay is committed to making human-centric products, such as prosthetics and household engineering solutions, more accessible. His vision is to revolutionize the field of robotics by enhancing linear actuators with soft robotics, aiming to create a significant impact in the industry.
